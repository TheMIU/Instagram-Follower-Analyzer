Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

This tool is a simple browser-based utility designed to help users compare their Instagram followers and following lists. It does not violate Instagram's terms of service and is not illegal in any way. No unauthorized access or automation is performed.

⚠️ Note: This tool does not connect to Instagram or make any automated requests. All data is manually input by the user and processed only in the browser.

What We Don’t Do

  • No data is collected, stored, or shared
  • No tracking cookies or analytics are used
  • No user login or account is required
  • No external servers are involved - everything runs locally

Data Handling

All data (text you paste or import) is handled entirely within your browser. It never leaves your device and is deleted when you refresh or close the page.
